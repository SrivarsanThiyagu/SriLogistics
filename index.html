About TeamEXP

Our Story

Team Express Pvt. Ltd. is one of the leading courier, cargo and logistics companies in India. Founded in 2019 with a mission to provide customers world-class courier services and professionalism.

We serve PAN India with established distribution alliances and partners, with networks in all major cities. Currently serving PAN India with a proven track record of 96% delivery attempt within 24-48 hours.

We provide integrated supply chain solutions by air, surface or rail routes. Our expertise along with our home-grown tech infrastructure enables clients to manage their flow of goods in real-time, making the entire delivery process transparent and easy to manage.
